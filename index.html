Storing & Logging

Ami only stores the necessary data in order to make it work. It savely stores your discord ID and your discord name. Those are stored because the bot have an economy system, which needs this things to works correctly: you can feel free to reach out the mod team, and apply to be removed from the database. Your information, data and other stuff are 100% secure, no one except the mod team can access to it, in any way.
